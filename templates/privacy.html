Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly: your account details such as name and email address, and the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information like your browser type for the purpose of keeping the service running.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the service — to display your expenses, generate summaries and category breakdowns, and keep your account secure. We do not sell your personal information or use your financial data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is associated with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorized access or loss. You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party providers for hosting and infrastructure. These providers process data only as needed to support the service and are expected to maintain appropriate safeguards. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We're happy to help.
